## Poster 1: "The Neural Gateway"

**Theme:** Futuristic recruitment / opportunity

**Visual concept:**
A massive glowing violet portal opens in the center of a dark cyberpunk cityscape. Through the portal, golden coins and data streams flow outward. A lone silhouette stands before it, reaching toward the light. The city is shrouded in mist, but the portal illuminates everything in deep purples and cyan.

**Headline:** *"Train the future. Earn your place."*

**Tagline:** "Complete AI tasks. Create with AI tools. Get paid."

**AI prompt (Ideogram / Midjourney / DALL-E):**
> A futuristic cyberpunk cityscape at night, dark with neon accents. Center: a massive glowing violet and cyan energy portal floating above the ground, emitting streams of golden particles and digital code. A silhouetted person stands before the portal, reaching toward it. Cinematic lighting, volumetric fog, purple and teal color scheme. Ultra-detailed, 8K, epic scale. Text overlay: "Train the future. Earn your place."

## Poster 2: "AI in Your Hands"

**Theme:** Creative empowerment / tools

**Visual concept:**
A pair of hands cupped open, holding a miniature glowing universe. Inside the universe: a paintbrush painting a galaxy, a music note radiating sound waves, a film reel unspooling into light, and a chat bubble filled with stars. The hands are realistic, the universe inside is luminous and magical. Dark background with light radiating from the center.

**Headline:** *"Create with AI."*

**Tagline:** "Image generation. Music studio. Video creation. AI Chat. All in one platform."

**AI prompt (Ideogram / Midjourney / DALL-E):**
> Realistic human hands cupped open, holding a miniature glowing cosmos in their palms. Inside the mini universe: a paintbrush creating a swirl of colors, a glowing music note with soundwave rings, a film strip winding through starlight, and a chat bubble with sparkling text. Cinematic lighting, deep dark background, warm golden light radiating from the center. Photorealistic hands, ethereal magical interior. 4K. Text: "Create with AI."

## Poster 3: "Earn While You Train"

**Theme:** Financial reward / gamification

**Visual concept:**
A dramatic upward graph made of glowing neon light, rising from the bottom-left to top-right. The graph line is constructed from interconnected icons: a clipboard, a star rating, a coin stack, a wallet, and a rocket at the peak. Digital particles float upward along the curve. Minimalist but energetic. Deep navy background with electric blue and gold accents.

**Headline:** *"Your effort. Your earnings. Your future."*

**Tagline:** "Paid AI training tasks. Fair rewards. Instant withdrawals."

**AI prompt (Ideogram / Midjourney / DALL-E):**
> A dramatic glowing neon line graph rising from bottom-left to top-right on a dark navy background. The graph line is made of connected icons: a clipboard, a five-star rating, a stack of coins, a wallet, and a rocket ship at the peak. Golden and electric blue particles float upward along the curve. Minimalist, clean, high-contrast, futuristic financial aesthetic. Cinematic lighting. 4K. Text: "Your effort. Your earnings. Your future."

## Poster 4: "The Trinity"

**Theme:** Three core offerings — Train, Create, Earn

**Visual concept:**
Three distinct vertical panels separated by thin neon dividers:
- **Left (Train):** A brain with neural network nodes, glowing violet
- **Center (Create):** A collage of creative tools — image of a mountain, a waveform, a film reel — glowing cyan
- **Right (Earn):** A rising chart with a wallet icon at the top, glowing gold
Each panel has its own color theme but they flow into each other at the edges. Dark background.

**Headline:** *"Train. Create. Earn."*

**Tagline:** "The all-in-one AI contributor platform."

**AI prompt (Ideogram / Midjourney / DALL-E):**
> A poster design with three vertical panels divided by thin neon lines. Left panel (violet): a stylized human brain with glowing neural network nodes and connecting lines. Center panel (cyan): a collage of creative tools — a photorealistic mountain landscape, a sound waveform, a film strip. Right panel (gold): a rising bar chart with a glowing wallet icon at the top. Dark background. Seamless gradient transitions between panels. High-tech aesthetic. Clean typography. 4K. Text: "Train. Create. Earn."

## Poster 5: "From Zero to Hero"

**Theme:** Transformation / journey

**Visual concept:**
Split composition. **Left side (before):** a dim, grayscale scene of a person looking at a laptop with a worried expression, empty wallet beside them, dull lighting. **Right side (after):** the same person, now brightly lit, smiling, surrounded by floating icons representing completed tasks, gold coins raining down, creative AI tools glowing around them. A diagonal crack of light splits the two halves. Warm vibrant colors on the right, cold desaturated on the left.

**Headline:** *"Turn your time into something bigger."*

**Tagline:** "Join thousands of contributors earning with TrainlyLabs."

**AI prompt (Ideogram / Midjourney / DALL-E):**
> Split composition poster. Left side: dim, grayscale, a person looking tired at a laptop with an empty wallet beside them, dull cold lighting, desaturated colors. Right side: same person now smiling and confident, laptop surrounded by floating task completion checkmarks, golden coins raining down, glowing AI tools (paint palette, music note, film reel) floating around them. A diagonal shatter line of golden light splits the two halves. Warm vibrant colors on right, cold and dark on left. Cinematic transformation feel. 4K. Text: "Turn your time into something bigger."

## Bonus: Color Palette Reference

Use these consistently across all posters for brand recognition:

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Violet | `#7c3aed` | Primary brand / AI / brain / neural |
| Cyan | `#06b6d4` | Creative tools / technology |
| Gold | `#f59e0b` | Earnings / rewards / success |
| Navy | `#0f172a` | Background |
| Dark | `#020617` | Deep background accents |

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter, or similar) — all caps or title case
- **Taglines:** Lightweight sans-serif, smaller size
- **Avoid** more than 2 font styles per poster
